Billy Willig

Stand up comedian, Musician, Podcaster

Billy Willig is a stand up comedian and bass player based in Atlanta, GA. At a young age Billy began taking youth theatre lessons and performed in community plays for Curtain Call youth Players and his local church, Legacy Church. There, a love for classical theatre was born. After high school Billy helped found a local pop punk band called LIKE MIKE. From there he toured the country playing songs and jumping on every stage that was available. Billy began doing stand up opening for bands or filling time slots at the local all-ages music venue. Some of Billy’s notable performances include Vans Warped Tour in Atlanta and opening for Alex Hooper (America’s Got Talent). Other notable acts Billy has opened for include Jordan Perry (Comedy Central, Hollywood Improv) and Dustin Sims (Popular YouTuber). Billy hopes to bring a fresh perspective as well as a respect for the arts to the stage with his observational humor and stories. In the future Billy hopes to perform in all 50 states and travel internationally to do stand up.
